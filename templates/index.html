RAFEK: AI Carbon Footprint Calculator.

RAFEK uses users expense data to figure out their footprint.

It magically creates reports in Word and Excel, so users don't have to write anything down. (high five!)

RAFEK analyzes users data and gives them cool ideas to shrink thier footprint, making them an eco-champ!

It also gives AI predictions to see the future (of your carbon footprint).

Built with : Python, Kivy & KivyMD, OpenAI API

This project helped me and my team grab 3rd place at the 7th UAE Hackathon! So it's not just cool, it's award-winning.

Learn Arabic HCT SJM: Telegram bot to learn Arabic.

Why This Bot was Created:
Picture this: You and your friends find yourselves enrolled in an Arabic course. Your collective Arabic vocabulary consists of "Assalamu alaikum" and "Shukran." But as a true engineer, you create a bot to make the course easier.

What It Does:
- Serves up quizzes tailored to your skill level.
- Awards points for correct answers.
- Ignites a competitive spirit among friends, turning language learning into a fun game.
- Makes the entire learning process so enjoyable, you might forget you're actually studying.

Built with : Python, Telebot

Shukran to this bot, now i can say أريد أن آكل (Uridu an aakul) (I want to eat). Let's be honest, being able to order food is a crucial life skill in any language.
